About DY Patil University

DY Patil University is a private university located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. The university was established in 2002 and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields such as medicine, engineering, management, and law.

The university is committed to providing high-quality education to its students and has a team of experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)
  • Master of Science (M.Sc)

Admissions

Admissions to DY Patil University are based on various entrance exams such as NEET, JEE, MHT CET, and CLAT. Students can apply online through the university's website or in person at the admission office.
